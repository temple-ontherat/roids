Asteroids Challenge

Press the button below to check out how I'm doing making an asteroids-type game.

Use the arrow keys on your keyboard to control the ship. Space bar to fire,

right and left arrows for turning, and up arrow for thrust. What I've done

recently is to add a score display, a kill countdown, and collision detection.

Basically, I've made it so you can play it as a game, which I'm really stoked about.

I hope you enjoy it, and let me know what you think.
